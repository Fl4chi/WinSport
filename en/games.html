Roulette

The King of Games

Our European Roulette brings you the pure excitement of the green table straight to your screen. The game is simple and elegant: a ball is launched onto a spinning wheel and you must guess where it will stop.

  • Straight Up: Bet on a specific number (0-36). The hardest bet but pays 35 times your stake!
  • Red or Black: Bet on the colour. Ideal for beginners, with almost 50% probability and an even payout (1:1).

Watch out for Zero (Green): if it comes up, all Red/Black and number bets lose.
